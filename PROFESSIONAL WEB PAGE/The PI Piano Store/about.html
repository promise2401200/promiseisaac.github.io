Our Story

The PI Piano Showroom has been serving the community since 1990. We are passionate about providing quality pianos and excellent customer service.

Welcome to My Piano Store, where exceptional music meets unparalleled craftsmanship.
Our curated collection boasts a wide array of pianos, from majestic grand pianos to versatile uprights, each meticulously crafted to offer superior sound quality and an impeccable playing experience
Whether you're just starting your musical journey or are an experienced pianist, our expert staff is dedicated to helping you find the ideal piano to match your aspirations.
At My Piano Store, we believe in the transformative power of music and are passionate about providing instruments that enhance every performance.
Our commitment extends beyond merely selling pianos; we offer personalized consultations, expert maintenance services, and a welcoming environment for all music enthusiasts.
We take pride in our comprehensive after-sales support, ensuring your piano remains in perfect condition for years to come.
Visit us today to explore the perfect blend of artistry and innovation, and let us help you bring the joy of music into your home
